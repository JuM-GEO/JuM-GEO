# Juliana Mantovani - Data and GIS Portfolio
Welcome! This repository hosts my personal portfolio and selected data science projects, with a strong focus on geospatial analysis.

## About Me
I am a GIS Specialist with over 10 years of experience working with geospatial data, environmental studies and spatial analysis. I am currently transitioning into Data Science, combining Python, GIS and statistical analysis to solve real-world problems.

## Featured Projects
### Melbourne Housing Market Analysis (GIS & Data Science)
An end-to-end data science project exploring Melbourne's rental housing market using open data, spatial analysis and predictive modeling.

**Technologies:** Python, Pandas, GeoPandas, Folium, Matplotlib, Scikit-learn
